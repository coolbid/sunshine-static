it is installed before and after the roll grooves to help guiding the steel accurately and steadily entering and exiting into and out of the roll groove.

Our roller guide are used in bar mill, high speed wire rod mills, as well as section bar mills (including angle bar channel bar, flat bar, H/I beam, T shaped bar, etc.)

Our products include all types of sliding entry guide, sliding exit guide, roller entry guide, roller exit guide, twist guide, and slitting guide(including second, third, fourth, fifth slitting), trimming guide etc. The roller guide also include 2-Hi，3-Hi，4-Hi series, which are used in bar mills, wire mills,section mills etc.

Our fourth/fifth slitting guide is in the leading level in China, it is renowned as China’s key new product, and China’s key energy saving product.

Our guide was sold to more than 100 steel companies in China, including Baosteel, Shougang Steel, Pinggang Steel etc.We have also provided designing and products to the wires, bars, section steel rolling companies in tens of countries.
